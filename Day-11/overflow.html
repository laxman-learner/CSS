Overflow:visible

By default , the overflow is visible, meaning that it is not clipped and it renders outside the element's box:

Overflow:scroll

Setting the overflow value to scroll, the overflow is clipped and a scrollbar is added to the scroll inside the box. This will add the scrollbar both horizontally and vertically

Overflow:auto

Setting the overflow value to auto, the overflow is clipped and a scrollbar is added to the scroll inside the box. This will add the scrollbar either horizontally or vertically when necessary

Overflow-x and overflow-y

We can also change the overflow of the content horizontally or vertically

overflow-x specifies what to do with the left/right edges of the contents.

overflow-y specifies what to do with the top/bottom edges of the contents.
